How It Works

From Distressed To Move-In Ready

Our proven 4-step process takes every property from neglected to neighborhood-ready — quickly, affordably, and built to last.

01
Property Identified

We source distressed or vacant residential properties through our network of brokers, auctions, and community partnerships across greater New Orleans.

02
Assessment & Acquisition

Our team evaluates structural condition, neighborhood demand, and renovation potential before moving forward with purchase and planning.

03
Full Renovation

A comprehensive gut renovation — new roofing, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, flooring, and modern finishes — completed to current code standards.

04
Listed & Leased

The property hits the market at an affordable rate. We screen applicants, sign leases, and welcome a new family into a home they can be proud of.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide every decision we make and every home we restore.

Our Mission
Revitalize. Restore. Return.

Our mission is to identify, acquire, and revitalize distressed residential properties across the greater New Orleans area, returning them to the market as safe, quality, and genuinely affordable housing for working families and individuals.

Every property we restore is a commitment to that belief. We do not just fill vacancies — we rebuild blocks, stabilize neighborhoods, and give families the foundation they need to thrive.

Operating with integrity, transparency, and a deep sense of local responsibility, we are committed to being a force for lasting positive change in the communities we call home.

Our Vision
Communities Where Everyone Belongs.

Our vision is a New Orleans where no residential property sits abandoned or underutilized when a family needs a home — a future where affordable housing is not a compromise, but a standard every community proudly upholds.

We envision Gibson Rental Realties, LLC as a model for community-centered real estate development where success is measured not only in profitability, but in lives improved and doors opened for families who deserve a better tomorrow.

By 2030, we aim to expand to 500+ revitalized units across the Gulf South, partnering with municipalities, nonprofits, and community organizations to multiply our impact.

Affordability First

Every unit we list is priced with working families in mind. Profitability is never pursued at the expense of accessibility.

Quality Without Compromise

We do not cut corners. Every renovation is thorough, structural, and built to provide years of safe, comfortable living.

Community Investment

We live here, work here, and invest here. Our properties are homes in neighborhoods we care deeply about.
